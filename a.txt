ACIP-X1
Automotive Cognitive Intelligence Platform
One-Line Definition

ACIP-X1 is an Agentic AI-powered automotive intelligence platform that connects engineering data, vehicle data, customer data, service data, and insurance processes through a unified Automotive Knowledge Graph.

1. Why ACIP-X1 Exists (5 min)
Current Automotive Industry Problems

Information is scattered across multiple systems:

Requirements → DOORS / Excel
Calibration → Excel
Source Code → Embedded C
CAN Signals → DBC
Testing → Excel
DTCs → Diagnostic Tools
Vehicle Data → ECU
Insurance → Separate Portals
Result

Engineers ask:

Why did the test fail?
Which requirement is affected?
What happens if calibration changes?

Customers ask:

Why is the check engine light ON?
Is my car safe to drive?
When should I service my vehicle?

Insurance teams ask:

What actually happened?
Was it a genuine claim?

Nobody has a single source of truth.
